## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will be able to:
- Understand the principles and benefits of Quality Management Systems
- Navigate ISO 9001 requirements and implement core QMS processes
- Apply industry-specific standards (AS9100, ISO 13485, IATF 16949)
- Develop quality documentation including procedures, work instructions, and control plans
- Conduct process validation and capability studies
- Manage supplier quality and incoming inspection
- Implement effective inspection and testing procedures
- Handle nonconforming product and execute CAPA processes
- Plan and conduct internal audits
- Drive continuous improvement using Lean and Six Sigma tools
- Prepare for and achieve third-party certification
- Utilize digital tools for quality management

## Prerequisites

- Module 21: Metrology and Precision Measurement
- Understanding of CNC machining processes
- Basic statistics knowledge
- Familiarity with engineering drawings and specifications
