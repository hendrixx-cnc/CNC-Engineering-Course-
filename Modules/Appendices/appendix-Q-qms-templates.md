
### 1.2 Quality Policy

```
Quality Policy Statement:

[Company Name] is committed to:
- Meeting or exceeding customer requirements and specifications
- Delivering defect-free products on time
- Continuously improving our processes and systems
- Complying with applicable regulatory and statutory requirements
- Maintaining a culture of quality throughout the organization

This policy is communicated to all employees and reviewed annually.

Signed: ___________________________  Date: __________
         (President/CEO)
```
