## C. Glossary of Terms

- **Backlash**: Lost motion due to clearance or play in mechanisms.
- **Datum**: Reference surface or line for measurement and alignment.
- **Feed rate**: Speed at which the tool moves through material.
- **Kerf**: Width of material removed by cutting process.
- **Servo**: Closed-loop motor system with encoder feedback.

## F. Safety Checklist

- Eye and hearing protection at all times
- Interlocked guards and E-stop circuits
- Fume extraction for plasma and laser
- Grounding and shielding for all electronics

## G. Troubleshooting Guide

- **No motion**: Check power, wiring, and drive enable.
- **Poor cut quality**: Verify tool sharpness, speed, and coolant.
- **Communication error**: Inspect cables and controller status.
- **Unusual noise**: Lubricate bearings and inspect for wear.
