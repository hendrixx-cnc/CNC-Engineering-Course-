## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will be able to:
- Understand the importance of work-life balance for health, performance, and longevity
- Implement proper ergonomics and physical health practices
- Manage stress and build mental resilience
- Navigate shift work and irregular schedules effectively
- Prioritize sleep, nutrition, and physical fitness
- Maintain strong family relationships and social connections
- Set and maintain healthy boundaries at work
- Plan for financial wellness and retirement
- Make career decisions that support life balance
- Create and execute a personal work-life balance plan
- Recognize when professional help is needed
- Build sustainable habits for long-term wellbeing

## Important Note

This module addresses work-life balance broadly, but individual circumstances vary greatly. The strategies presented should be adapted to personal situations, family dynamics, health conditions, and workplace realities. When facing serious health issues (physical or mental), always consult qualified healthcare professionals.

Work-life balance is a journey, not a destination. It requires continuous attention, adjustment, and commitment. The goal is sustainable wellbeing over a long career, not perfection at any given moment.
