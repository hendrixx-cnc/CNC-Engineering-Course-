- **Safety:** Always include safety considerations in practical sections
- **Cost:** Provide order-of-magnitude cost estimates where applicable

### Technical Writing Standards

- **Equations:** Introduce purpose before presenting formula
- **Derivations:** Show intermediate steps, not just final result
- **Examples:** State "Given," "Calculate," "Solution," "Result" structure
- **Tables:** Use markdown tables with alignment
- **Lists:** Use bullet points for items, numbered lists for procedures
- **Cross-references:** Link related sections within module
