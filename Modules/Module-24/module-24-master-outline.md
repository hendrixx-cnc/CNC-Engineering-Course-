## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will be able to:
- Understand core L.E.A.N. principles and their application to CNC manufacturing
- Identify and eliminate the eight wastes in manufacturing
- Create value stream maps and design future states
- Implement Just-In-Time and pull production systems
- Design and operate Kanban systems
- Apply SMED to reduce setup times dramatically
- Implement Total Productive Maintenance (TPM)
- Design and operate manufacturing cells
- Create error-proofing solutions (Poka-Yoke)
- Lead and participate in Kaizen events
- Establish standardized work and visual management
- Develop L.E.A.N. metrics and management systems
- Lead L.E.A.N. transformation initiatives
- Adapt L.E.A.N. tools for job shop environments

## Prerequisites

- Module 23: Shop Organization and Management
- Module 22: Quality Management Systems
- Understanding of CNC manufacturing processes
