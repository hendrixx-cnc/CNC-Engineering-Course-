## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, students will be able to:
- Assess personal readiness for business ownership
- Create a comprehensive business plan for a CNC shop
- Understand legal structures and choose the right entity
- Set up and manage accounting and financial systems
- Navigate tax requirements and maximize deductions
- Calculate accurate job costs and set profitable pricing
- Evaluate, finance, and purchase equipment strategically
- Build and manage vendor relationships effectively
- Hire, train, and retain quality employees
- Develop effective marketing and sales strategies
- Manage operations efficiently and profitably
- Understand realistic expectations for various scenarios (best, worst, normal)
- Identify and mitigate business risks
- Plan for growth and scale strategically
- Make informed decisions about business ownership

## Important Disclaimer

This module provides general guidance on business ownership. Laws, regulations, and best practices vary by location and change over time. Always consult with qualified professionals (attorneys, accountants, business advisors) for specific advice related to your situation. The authors and publisher are not responsible for business decisions made based on this educational content.

Business ownership involves significant financial risk. Many businesses fail, especially in the first few years. Ensure you understand the risks and have adequate financial resources before starting a business.
